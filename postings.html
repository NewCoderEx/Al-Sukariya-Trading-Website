Housemaid

Full Time

Responsibilities include cleaning and sanitizing rooms, dusting, vacuuming, laundry, and ensuring overall household tidiness. Housemaids may also assist with meal preparation, basic home organization, and occasionally caring for household pets. A keen attention to detail, reliability, and a strong work ethic are crucial for success in this role.

  • Experience:

    Previous experience in domestic work or as a housemaid.

  • Adaptability:

    Ability to adapt to different cultural environments and work conditions.

  • Communication Skills:

    Clear communication skills to understand and follow instructions.

  • Legal Compliance:

    Willingness and ability to meet legal requirements for working abroad, including obtaining necessary permits.

  • Physical Fitness:

    Good health and stamina to handle the physical demands of the job.

  • References:

    Positive references from previous employers highlighting reliability and work ethic.

  • Language Proficiency:

    Basic proficiency in the local language, if applicable.

  • Cultural Sensitivity:

    Respect for cultural norms and traditions.

  • Background Check:

    Clean record and ability to pass background checks.
