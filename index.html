About

Al-Sukariya Trading is a recruitment agency since 1984 and deployed almost thousands of applicants abroad.

Our Vision

We are dedicated to fostering mutually beneficial partnerships between skilled individuals and visionary organizations, creating a powerful synergy that not only meets immediate needs but also fuels long-term growth. With a commitment to precision, integrity, and innovation, we aspire to be the catalyst for positive change in the careers of individuals and the prosperity of businesses, leaving an indelible mark on the evolving world of workforce solutions.

Our Approach

Our license allows us to recruit and deploy housemaid, caregivers, waiters, waitress, beauticians, male and female cleaners, baby sitters and all other human services. We, actively search, recruit and screen candidates for employment and train them according to specific client requirements and deploy to their work locations. The candidates must pass through screening process before referring for acceptance. Our recruitment process is very simple and efficient where quality and clients satisfaction is our prime motto.
